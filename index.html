Don't Overthink is a classic run-and-gun action game focused heavily on mental health. Inspired by cartoons from the 1930s, the visuals and audio are painstakingly created using the same techniques of the time: traditional drawn animation . hand-drawn, watercolor backgrounds and original jazz recordings.
